# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Retail counter staff, wholesale operators, inventory handlers, and admins use this desktop POS during active shop operations. They need fast billing, accurate stock movement, clear invoice history, and trustworthy daily reporting without extra visual noise.

## Product Purpose

Spice POS manages retail and wholesale billing, packet and bulk inventory, customer credit, expenses, invoice history, labels, and business reports. Success means transactions are quick to enter, stock and credit stay correct, and owners can understand sales, collections, expenses, and profit at a glance.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, practical. The interface should feel like a dependable shop-floor tool: familiar enough for repeated use, polished enough to inspire trust, and restrained enough that numbers stay central.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative dashboard theatrics, marketing-style hero layouts, oversized rounded cards, heavy glass effects, gratuitous animation, and chart colors that compete with the business data. Do not make the app feel like a generic SaaS landing page or a dense spreadsheet clone.

## Design Principles

1. Put transaction accuracy ahead of decoration.
2. Make totals, stock, credit, and exceptions easy to scan.
3. Use familiar controls for billing, filters, tables, and reports.
4. Keep visual emphasis tied to status, action, or data meaning.
5. Preserve speed for counter workflows and admin review.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target clear contrast for text and chart labels, keyboard-visible focus states, reduced-motion respect, and color choices that remain understandable with labels or values when hue is not distinguishable.
